About us

"Why Think When You Can Rote? Join the Notes Parade and Skip the Concepts!"

In my first semester, I struggled to find notes and wasted hours searching. To save others that hassle, I created this website as a central hub for all the notes you need. But remember, real learning happens through understanding concepts, not just memorizing notes. Use these notes to help with exams, but focus on the bigger picture. If you have notes to share, contribute and help others succeed too!
